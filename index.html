Bitcoin

Bitcoin is the first and most widely known cryptocurrency. It is often considered digital gold due to its limited supply and store of value properties.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized platform that enables smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps) to be built and run.

Binance Coin

Binance Coin is the native cryptocurrency of the Binance Exchange, one of the largest cryptocurrency exchanges in the world.

Cardano

Cardano is a blockchain platform that aims to provide a more balanced and sustainable ecosystem for cryptocurrencies through its layered architecture.

Solana

Solana is a highly functional open source project that banks on blockchain technology's permissionless nature to provide DeFi solutions.

Polkadot

Polkadot is a multi-chain network that aims to enable different blockchains to interoperate seamlessly. Strong focus on interoperability, scalability.
